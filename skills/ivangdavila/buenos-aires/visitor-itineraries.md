# Buenos Aires Visitor Itineraries

## 1-Day Itinerary: The Essentials

### Morning (9am-1pm)
**Recoleta**
1. **9:00** - Cementerio de la Recoleta (1.5h)
   - Find Evita's tomb
   - Marvel at the architecture
   - Go early to avoid crowds
2. **10:30** - Coffee at La Biela
   - Historic cafe across from cemetery
   - Watch the scene
3. **11:30** - Walk to El Ateneo Grand Splendid
   - 15 min walk down Av. Santa Fe
   - Bookstore in former theater
   - Photos + browse

### Lunch (1pm-3pm)
**Options:**
- **El Sanjuanino** (Recoleta): Quick empanadas
- **Walk to Palermo Soho** (20 min): Better restaurant options
- **Recommended:** Walk through Recoleta parks to Palermo

### Afternoon (3pm-7pm)
**Palermo**
1. **3:00** - Palermo Soho exploration
   - Boutique shopping
   - Street art
   - Coffee break
2. **5:00** - Japanese Garden or Rosedal
   - Beautiful parks
   - Peaceful break
3. **6:30** - Sunset walk in Bosques de Palermo

### Evening (8pm onward)
**Dinner + Nightlife**
1. **8:30** - Walk around Palermo Hollywood
2. **9:30** - Dinner at Don Julio (book ahead) or La Cabrera
3. **11:30** - Cocktails at Victoria Brown or 878
4. **Optional:** Live music or milonga

- Coffee at historic cafe
- Mercado de San Telmo (covered)
- Cooking class
